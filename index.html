📖 How to Play
🎯 Gameplay
Top-down football against the computer. First to 7 goals wins the match. Your ball speed stays the same for the whole match.

🕹️ Controls
Left lane – drag up/down to move your goalkeeper.
Right lane – drag up/down to move your striker.
Use two fingers at once to control both.
Tap near the ball to hit a power shot.
Flick the paddle as it meets the ball to change the angle – the ball keeps its speed, only its direction changes.

⚽ Formation
Your striker plays in the opponent's half, in front of their goal. Their striker sits in front of yours. Goalkeepers stay on the goal line.

✨ Bonus ball
A small extra ball sometimes crosses the pitch. Hit it for a random effect lasting a few seconds – it may help you or favour the CPU. This will be replaced by the Shop later.

📈 Stages & seasons
100 stages, grouped into 10 seasons of 10 matches.
Inside a season the ball gets faster with every stage. At the start of a new season it slows back down – but the CPU gets sharper: it aims better, reacts faster, and starts reading where the ball will land.

⭐ Stars
Concede 0–1 goals: ★★★
Concede 2–4 goals: ★★☆
Concede 5–6 goals: ★☆☆
Your best result per stage is kept.
