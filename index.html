📖 How to Play
🎯 Gameplay
Top-down football against the computer. First to 7 goals wins the match.

🕹️ Controls
Left lane — drag up/down to move your keeper.
Right lane — drag up/down to move your striker.
Use two fingers at once to control both.
Flick a paddle as it meets the ball to change the angle — the ball keeps its speed, only its direction changes.

⚡ Power shot
Tap anywhere near the ball to fire a power shot — the nearest paddle strikes it hard. Watch for the ⚡ TAP marker.

🔥 Rally
Passing the ball back and forth forever won't save you. After 10 touches the ball starts speeding up with every hit, up to 1.8×. A goal resets it.

⚽ Formation
Your striker plays in the opponent's half, in front of their goal. Their striker sits in front of yours. Keepers stay on the goal line.

📈 Stages & seasons
100 stages, grouped into 10 seasons of 10 matches.
Inside a season the ball gets faster with every stage. At the start of a new season it slows back down — but the CPU gets sharper: it aims better, reacts faster, and starts reading where the ball will land.

⭐ Stars
Concede 0–1 goals: ★★★
Concede 2–4 goals: ★★☆
Concede 5–6 goals: ★☆☆
Your best result per stage is kept.

💰 Coins
You earn coins for every win. First clears pay the most — replaying a stage you've already beaten pays much less. More stars, more coins. Later seasons pay more.

🛒 Shop
Spend coins on boosts. Buy them in the Shop, then tap them during a match from the bar at the bottom.
Up to 3 activations per match.
Most boosts last until the next goal (max 30s).
Pro boosts (120) last the whole match — they are the way out when you're stuck.
💰 Double coins is not a boost: it pays out on your next win, and it's kept if you lose.

👥 2 Player
Two players, one device — one on each side.
Pick your pitch before every match. The pitch you choose also sets the tempo: ⚡ calm, ⚡⚡⚡ wild. New pitches unlock as you progress in Stage mode.
Bonus balls cross the pitch during play — catch one with your keeper for a random effect. What you got is shown at the top of the screen.
The Shop is Stage mode only: one device, one coin balance — buying boosts here wouldn't be a fair match.
What should we call you?
Shown on the pitch and in your results. You can change it later.
