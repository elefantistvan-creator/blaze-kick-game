📖 How to Play
🎯 Gameplay
Blaze Kick is an air hockey game against the computer. Whoever reaches the goal count needed to win first (see the start screen) wins the match. The ball speeds up a little after every goal.

🕹️ Controls
Left lane – drag up/down to move the goalkeeper.
Right lane – drag up/down to move the field player.
Use two fingers at once to control both.
Tap near the ball with your paddle for a power shot.
Hitting the ball while moving fast with a quick flick adds spin.

✨ Bonus ball
A small extra ball occasionally appears on the field. Hit it to trigger a random effect for a few seconds – it can be helpful (e.g. bigger opponent goal, faster shots, bigger field player) or harmful (e.g. your own goal grows, or your field player shrinks) – react fast!

📈 Stage progression
Every match you win raises the stage by one – the ball and the CPU both get a bit stronger (+2.5% per stage). Your progress is saved, so you continue right where you left off next time.
